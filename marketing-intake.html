How It Works -- 5 Steps
1

Request Submitted

Anyone in the business submits a marketing request through a single form on the Grow platform. No more emails, Teams messages, or walk-ups.

2

Quality Gate

AI checks the brief for 4 required fields: task type, due date, target audience, and call to action. Incomplete briefs are bounced back automatically.

3

Agent Routing

Valid briefs are decomposed into sub-tasks and routed to specialised AI agents: copy, design, social, email. Each agent produces a first draft.

4

Human Review

Every piece of output is reviewed and approved by a human before it goes live. The AI does the production work. The team does the quality control.

5

Output Shipped

Approved content is published. Performance is tracked. The data loops back to make agents better next time.
