## What Went Into It -- The Behind-the-Scenes

This is not just output -- it is architecture. Every piece of work above involved:

- **Discovery before building** -- every team member got a session to map their workflow before any tool was spec'd. No wasted builds.
- **Parallel stakeholder coverage** -- Marketing, Sales, and BD all scoped within 3 weeks. 13+ sessions across all functions.
- **Infrastructure first** -- MCPs connected (Databricks, Asana, M365) before builds that depend on them were started. Clean foundation.
- **Skill system built as a product** -- not just documents. SKILL.md files are structured for Claude.ai personal apps. Reusable, role-specific, and handed off in 1:1s so people actually use them.
- **Proof-of-concept before pitch** -- Brand Intelligence prototype built before approaching Steve. NDIS Brain scoped with the subject matter expert before a line of code.
- **Live automations counted conservatively** -- only counting tools a real person is actively using. Not inflating the KPI.
