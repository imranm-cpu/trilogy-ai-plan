The AI layer is not a tech initiative. It is the commercial mechanism that makes a leaner, higher-output Growth function viable at scale.

The Case for AI Across Growth

Trilogy's FY27 forecast targets 7,082 net new B2C customers from a $5.568M marketing budget at $95 CPL. Achieving those numbers with 42% fewer people requires the AI layer to be fully operational — not as a support function, but embedded in every revenue-generating workflow. McKinsey benchmarks the incremental productivity opportunity from GenAI in sales and marketing at $0.8–1.2 trillion globally. The companies capturing that value are not running pilots — they are redesigning core business processes. That is the intent here.

Commercial Reference

The efficiency case is grounded in the FY27 forecast: a $5.568M marketing budget targeting 7,082 net new B2C customers at $95 CPL. The $500K+ savings figure represents headcount reduction net of two new hires plus efficiency gains reinvested into acquisition — approximately 9% operating leverage improvement against total budget. The IDC benchmark is $3.70 returned per $1 invested. The ambition here is the top 5%: $10 per $1, driven by agents embedded in core revenue-generating workflows — not support functions.
